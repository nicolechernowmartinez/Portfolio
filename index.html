Agentics Growth Lab

Product Thinking Growth Console

Executive Summary

The Product Thinking Growth Console translates the NCM product framework into a measurable operational intelligence system. Instead of treating product judgment as static documentation, the console structures discovery, prioritization, scoring, recommendations, and iteration into one executive-ready workspace.

The Problem

Product and operations teams in fintech and regulated environments often make high-impact decisions across fragmented artifacts: roadmaps, meeting notes, KPI dashboards, risk conversations, customer feedback, and delivery backlogs. The result is not a lack of strategy — it is a lack of operating telemetry that connects strategy to measurable product movement.

The Solution

  • Product Thinking Growth workspace to organize hypotheses, experiments, product decisions, and leadership-ready narratives
  • Telemetry-driven scoring to evaluate signal strength, adoption movement, friction, risk, and iteration maturity
  • Recommendation workflows that propose next actions without removing human judgment or decision rights
  • Measure + Iteration workspaces that connect product decisions to observable outcomes over time

Operating Model

Signal Capture — Product, customer, operations, and delivery signals organized
Scoring — Telemetry creates a measurable view of priority, friction, and risk
Recommendation — AI proposes next-best actions and decision options
Human Review — Product leaders validate context and business judgment
Iteration — Measure loops confirm whether decisions improved the outcome

Positioning: AI does not own the decision. The console makes product thinking measurable, repeatable, and visible.

Five9 Call Center Integration for Loan Origination Platform

Executive Summary

Designed and delivered a real-time integration between Five9's cloud call-center platform and a home-grown loan origination system — transforming voice calls from isolated support interactions into fully connected financial workflow events.

Key Initiatives

1. Embedded Call Panel in the Loan Platform

Native call management interface directly inside the LOS — incoming call pop-ups, one-click call controls, live loan details, and inline notes stored to the loan record.

Impact: Eliminated system switching for agents entirely.

2. Automatic Call-to-Loan Linking

Every call produced a structured record automatically — Loan ID, Borrower ID, Agent ID, Timestamp, Call type, and Outcome.

Impact: Complete interaction history per loan, without manual data entry.

3. Compliance and Audit Trail Engine

Structured call metadata in a queryable format designed for regulatory review.

Impact: Simplified regulatory audits and reduced compliance team effort per review cycle.

Idea Capture — Voice to Structured Document

✦ Try Idea Capture Live

What It Does

Idea Capture removes the friction between having an idea and having a document. Speak for up to 10 minutes — stream of consciousness, half-formed thoughts, rough framing — and Claude organizes it into a clean, structured artifact you can download and share immediately.

The Problem It Solves

The best ideas often happen in motion — in a car, between meetings, on a walk. By the time you open a document, the energy is gone. Idea Capture meets you where the idea is: your voice.

Speak — Record up to 10 minutes directly in the browser. No app, no login.
Submit — Claude receives your transcript and organizes your thoughts into structured sections.
Download — A clean document appears within 10–20 seconds, ready to share or develop further.

Core principle: AI should reduce the distance between insight and artifact — not add steps. This tool demonstrates that philosophy in the simplest possible form.

Technology

  • Cloudflare Workers — serverless edge deployment, zero cold starts
  • Claude API — real AI organizing and structuring spoken content
  • Browser Speech API — no native app required, works in the browser
  • Serverless architecture — scales to any volume without infrastructure management
